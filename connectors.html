Connectors Practice Exercises

1. Sequencing Linkers

Connectors to use: first, second, then, next, finally
Every school day I wake up at 7 o’clock. , I get out of bed and open the curtains. , I go to the bathroom and wash my face. , I brush my teeth and comb my hair. , I go to the kitchen and eat breakfast quickly. , I take my school bag and leave the house.

2. Addition Linkers

Connectors to use: also, moreover, in addition, besides, furthermore
Weekends are the best days of the week. , I can sleep late and relax. , I meet my friends and go for a walk. , I watch films or play video games at home. , I help my mum in the kitchen sometimes. , I feel happy and full of energy after the weekend.

3. Contrast Linkers

Connectors to use: however, although, but, nevertheless, on the other hand
I love a big breakfast with eggs and toast. , my sister prefers only coffee and a small croissant. , she says it’s faster in the morning. , I think a big breakfast gives more energy. , she never feels hungry before lunch. , I sometimes try her way when I’m in a hurry.

4. Cause and Effect Linkers

Connectors to use: because, so, therefore, as a result, consequently
Last year I drank a lot of sweet juice every day. , I felt tired very often. , my mum told me to drink more water. , I started drinking two litres of water every day. , I have much more energy now. , I feel healthier and my skin looks better.

5. Example Linkers

Connectors to use: for example, for instance, such as, namely, in particular
There are many healthy foods I eat regularly. , I eat a lot of fruit every day. , apples and bananas are my favourites. , vegetables like carrots and tomatoes are very good for me. , dark chocolate with 70% cocoa is a healthy sweet option. , I try to avoid sweets with too much sugar.
